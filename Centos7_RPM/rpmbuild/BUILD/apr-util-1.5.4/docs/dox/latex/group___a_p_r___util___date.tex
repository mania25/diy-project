Parses a string resembling an R\-F\-C 822 date. This is meant to be leinent in its parsing of dates. Hence, this will parse a wider range of dates than apr\-\_\-date\-\_\-parse\-\_\-http.

The prominent mailer (or poster, if mailer is unknown) that has been seen in the wild is included for the unknown formats.

    Sun, 6 Nov 1994 08:49:37 GMT   ; RFC 822, updated by RFC 1123
